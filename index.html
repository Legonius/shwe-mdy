About Us

History

  • Company was established in 2021 by Mr. Win Zaw Myat with a vision to provide the valued engineering in construction market.
  • We provide value-added construction services to our customers by implementing value engineering, upgrade construction technology & methodology, sustainable design, satisfied quality and safe environment.
  • We build trust with our business partners by sharing value, knowledge, technology and methodology.
  • We commit ourselves that we will build mutually satisfying long term relationship.

Our Vision

Shwe Mandalay Construction is to be the best service provider in myanmar with excellent solution methods, quality products and international best standard.

Our Mission

  • To implement the best business strategy to hit our vision step by step.
  • To create a better everyday life for our partnerships.
  • To help bring creative projects to our customers.

Why Choose Shwe Mandalay Construction ?

Our business plan and our culture focused on being the best, not the biggest construction company. we are proud to serve some of the most respected clients in Myanmar. While our company is new, we have a small company feel and caring approach. We take great pride in getting to know our associates and understanding what is important to them in their professional and their personal lives.
